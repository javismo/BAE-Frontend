What is Local Digital Twins & Data Spaces?

Local Digital Twins & Data Spaces is a comprehensive online catalogue of cloud and edge offerings, available through a collection of independent marketplaces and different product and service providers.

The uniqueness of the Local Digital Twins & Data Spaces is its ability to generate and use verifiable credentials ensuring the publication of trusted services, according to EU regulation. In other words, it enables a standardised process to verify that services and providers are certified and trustable.

Among its benefits, Local Digital Twins & Data Spaces offers several advantages to users:

Provides trusted and standardised access to service providers’ offers, matching customers’ demands.

Ensures verified and comparable qualifications across services and products.

Facilitates simple and transparent transactions and service management.

Why choose Local Digital Twins & Data Spaces?

Local Digital Twins & Data Spaces, with this single aggregated catalogue of products delivered by pre-validated European suppliers, aims to streamline the procurement journey, improve vendor selection, reduce search and evaluation time, facilitate product comparison, accelerate purchasing processes and simplify spending analysis, benefiting both buyers and suppliers within the European ecosystem.

Local Digital Twins & Data Spaces: A central piece of Europe’s Digital future.

Local Digital Twins & Data Spaces is aligned with Europe’s vision of a digital future powered by cloud computing. The marketplace has been deployed by the Local Digital Twins & Data Spaces project, as part of the European Commission’s strategy to co-invest with Member States in the development of new digital infrastructures, as well as to improve existing capabilities at both national and EU levels.

The goal is to provide European companies and public organisations with the single-point access to trusted cloud and edge services, necessary for digital transformation. In this context, Local Digital Twins & Data Spaces has been designed to offer a better and greater availability of trusted cloud and edge services, offered by providers and marketplaces members of the Local Digital Twins & Data Spaces ecosystem,

focusing on increasing the adoption of these services among public and private entities, particularly in sectors that handle sensitive data, and who may be lagging behind in cloud adoption. This marketplace is a fundamental part of the EU’s objective to strengthen open strategic digital autonomy and boost Europe’s digital future.
